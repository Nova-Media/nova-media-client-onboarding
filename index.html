What to expect

Honest timelines, clear communication, and no surprises. Here's exactly how the first 30 days look.

Launch Timeline

Day 1–3

Setup & Build

We review your intake form, build creative, configure your CRM pipeline, and connect your ad accounts.

Day 4–7

Campaign Goes Live

Your ads launch. The first week is a learning phase — Meta's algorithm is optimizing for your ideal lead profile. Volume may be low early.

Week 2–3

Leads Start Flowing

Quality and volume will vary early — this is completely normal. Speed of follow-up is critical during this phase.

Week 4+

Optimization & Scale

We cut what's not working and scale what is. Results compound as the algorithm matures and data builds.

  • Respond to new leads within 5 minutes — speed is the #1 conversion factor
  • Expect fluctuation in lead quality during the first 14 days — this is normal
  • We'll send you regular updates and performance reports without you having to ask
